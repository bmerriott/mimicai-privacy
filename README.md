# MimicAI Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

## Overview

MimicAI ("the app") is a local-first AI companion app. By default, all AI processing runs entirely on your device. No account is required to use the app.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- We do not have servers that receive your data
- We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind
- We do not sell data to third parties

---

## Microphone Access

The app requests microphone permission solely for voice input (speech-to-text). Voice audio is processed entirely on-device using a local speech recognition model. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

---

## Cloud AI Providers (Optional)

MimicAI defaults to running AI models locally on your device. You may optionally configure a third-party cloud AI provider (such as Groq) by entering your own API key in the app settings.

**When a cloud provider is configured:**
- Your conversation text is sent to that provider's servers for processing
- This transmission occurs directly from your device to the provider's API
- It is subject to that provider's own privacy policy
- Your API key is stored locally on your device using Android's secure Keystore system and is never transmitted to us

You can switch back to local processing at any time in Settings.

---

## API Keys

Any API keys you enter are stored exclusively on your device using the Android Keystore. They are never sent to our servers or any third party other than the provider you configured.

---

## Local Storage

AI model files and app settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete them at any time via the app's Settings screen or by uninstalling the app.

---

## Children's Privacy

MimicAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
